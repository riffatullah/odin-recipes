Qabuli Polao

The dish which has so popular in Afghanistan that it is considered as a national dish of the country. Emerging from Afghanistan, this dish is also popular among the Pashtun people of Pakistan. Kabuli Pulao is a rice dish consisting of steamed rice mixed with raisins, carrots, and lamb. This dish may be made with lamb, chicken, or beef & maybe decorated with raisins, orange peel strips, and chopped nuts like pistachios or almonds.

Ingredients

  • Beef/Mutton(Boiled)
  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Onions
  • Black Pepper
  • Cummins Power
  • Cummins
  • Salt
  • Raisins
  • Carrots
